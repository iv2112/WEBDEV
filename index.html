WEBDEV

WEBDEV has been made to allow beginners to explore and get interested in the field of Website Development. It introduces the subject, tells its importance in the tech world, lists various job opportunities along with their salaries, and all the courses needed to learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript in one place.

This website will give you all the details which you will want to know before you dive into the field of Website Development. You can easily click the buttons above to see the details of a certain topic which is related to Website Developemt and how learning it can be useful to you in the future and be a powerful skill.

The following webpages are designed to help you know more about Website Development. They are Introduction, Importance, Jobs, Salaries, and Courses, to help you know everything about making websites, why this skill holds importance, and get you started on your path to learning more programming languages.
